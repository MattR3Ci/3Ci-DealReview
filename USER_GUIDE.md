# User Guide: 3Ci Deal Review (Deal Board)

## App Intent
The Deal Review application is the formal gateway for all 3Ci sales opportunities. It provides a structured workflow for sales leaders to submit deals, ensures all required commercial and legal documents are present, and leverages AI to flag potential risks before a deal is signed.

## Screen-by-Screen Guide

### 1. Deal Board (Home)
The central command center for all deals.
- **My Active Deals:** Track the status of your submissions (Draft, Submitted, Approved, Rejected).
- **Review Queue:** High-level overview of deals pending review (for Reviewers).
- **KPIs:** Real-time metrics on total deal value and throughput.

### 2. Deal Submission Form
The "Step-by-Step" wizard.
- **Basics:** Deal title, client selection, and commercial model.
- **Commercials:** Financial details, payment terms, and billing frequency.
- **Roles:** Assign Sales Leaders, Accountable Executives, and Delivery Leads.
- **Ready-to-Submit:** A real-time "Readiness Ring" shows what fields or documents are still missing.

### 3. Document Governance (SharePoint)
- **Automatic Provisioning:** The app creates a secure SharePoint folder for every new deal.
- **Document Matrix:** The app tells you exactly which documents (SOW, MSA, Cost Model) are required based on the deal's commercial model.
- **AI Health Check:** Uploaded documents are automatically scanned for compliance.

### 4. Reviewer Workspace
- **Risk Assessment:** Reviewers see AI-flagged risks and can override them with justifications.
- **Decision Panel:** Formally Approve or Reject a deal with timestamped notes.

## Common Workflows
- **Submitting a Deal:**
  1. Create a new deal on the Deal Board.
  2. Fill in all required fields.
  3. Go to the Workspace and upload required documents.
  4. Wait for the AI scan to complete.
  5. Click "Submit for Review" once the Readiness Ring hits 100%.
- **Handling a Rejection:** Review the rejection notes, update the fields or documents as requested, and click "Submit" again to restart the review workflow.
